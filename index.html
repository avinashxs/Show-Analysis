Phase 1: Casting a Wide Net

The first step is to aggressively filter the entire event landscape using strict, non-negotiable criteria. This initial pass removes the vast majority of irrelevant events, focusing our resources effectively.

Core Screening Filters

  • 📍
    Location: Strictly Follow Location
  • 💼
    Event Type: Strictly B2B Exhibitions
  • 🚫
    Exclusions: No Webinars, Virtual, or Public Events

Filtering Impact

This initial screening is designed to be ruthless, eliminating up to 95% of events to isolate the most promising candidates for deep-dive analysis.

Phase 2: Qualifying the Venue

Once a show passes the initial screen, we perform a deep-dive analysis. We use hard quantitative metrics for size and scale, combined with qualitative assessments of audience and influence to determine if a show is truly worth pursuing.

    Attendee Profile: Presence of real buyers & international participants.

    Speakers: Influential & relevant industry leaders.

    Buzz & Engagement: High social media activity (LinkedIn, Instagram) indicating strong interest.

Show Pursuit Decision

Based on the comprehensive analysis, each show is categorized for its pursuit potential.

Phase 3: Pinpointing Ideal Exhibitors

With a shortlist of high-potential shows, we then dive into identifying specific exhibitor companies that align with our strategic objectives, using a multi-faceted filtering approach.

Universal Exhibitor Filters

Before segmenting by size, we apply two key filters to identify active and relevant companies within any show.

💻

Official Participation

Must be listed as an official exhibitor or sponsor in past or present editions of the show.

📱

Social Media Activity

Company actively promotes their show participation on LinkedIn, Instagram, or Facebook.
